About Exam Portal

The Exam Portal is a secure and user-friendly platform designed to simplify online student examinations. It enables teachers to create, manage, and evaluate exams, while students can easily register, log in, and take their assessments from any location.

Features include user authentication for students and teachers, dashboard views, exam creation and submissions, real-time monitoring, and result management.

Our goal is to bring accessibility, efficiency, and integrity to the online examination process.
